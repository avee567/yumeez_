Agene Nancy — CEO & Founder

Hi! I'm Nancy, the founder of Yumeez Pastries. From a young age, I’ve had a passion for baking and creativity in the kitchen. What started as a small treat for friends and family has grown into a full-fledged business that brings smiles to many.

My goal is to make every pastry a little masterpiece — full of flavor, warmth, and joy. I believe in quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction. Yumeez Pastries is more than a business to me; it's a way to share happiness with the world.

Thank you for being part of our journey!
