Our Story

Yumeez Pastries started as a small kitchen dream, born from a young woman’s passion to serve delicious, handcrafted treats that bring joy to people’s lives. Nancy, the founder, began baking for her family and neighbors during her teenage years.

Word spread quickly about her flavorful pastries, warm attitude, and attention to detail. Over time, she built what is now known as Yumeez Pastries — a brand that stands for taste, love, and consistency.

From humble beginnings to a growing customer base, we are proud of our roots and excited for the future. We believe every bite should be an experience — and every customer, a part of our family.

Thank you for believing in us.
